Cars & Coffee.

Eight in the morning. The Dilly Dally lot. A hundred cars, a hundred conversations, a good pour of Thump to start the day.

How it works

Show up, park up, pour up.

Bend Cars & Coffee runs the 1st and 3rd Sundays of every month from 8 to 10 am, rain or shine. The lot opens at 7:45. It is free, there is no registration, and every make and model is welcome. We see classic rally cars parked next to new electric builds parked next to weekend wagons parked next to exotics. It is good.

Aaron Hofferber of Avants hosts, we pour the coffee, and the community brings the cars. No gatekeeping, no judging, no entry fee. Roll in, grab a drink, walk the lot.

The ground rules

Keep it clean.

  • Arrive by 7:45 if you want a prime spot. Lot fills fast.
  • Kill the motor when you park. Fire it up on the way out, not while people are chatting.
  • No burnouts, no donuts, no drifting in the lot. Emkay Dr is public road.
  • Pack it in, pack it out. There is a trash can by the door.
  • Be cool to the neighbors. The lot is shared with other businesses.
  • Say hi to Jack and the baristas. That is the whole point.
