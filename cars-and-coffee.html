Cars & Coffee.

Eight in the morning. The Dilly Dally lot. A hundred cars, a hundred conversations, a good pour of Thump to start the day.

Show up, park up, pour up. Bend Cars & Coffee runs the 1st and 3rd Sundays of every month from 8 to 10 am, rain or shine.

The lot opens at 7:45. It is free, there is no registration, and every make and model is welcome. We see classic rally cars parked next to new electric builds parked next to weekend wagons parked next to exotics. It is good.

Aaron Hofferber of Avants hosts, we pour the coffee, and the community brings the cars. No gatekeeping, no judging, no entry fee. Roll in, grab a drink, walk the lot.

Keep it clean

The ground rules.

Arrive early.

Lot opens at 7:45. Best spots go fast. No stress if you roll up at 9.

Kill the motor.

When you park, shut it off. Fire it up on the way out, not while people are chatting.

No burnouts.

No donuts, no drifting in the lot. Emkay Dr is a public road, we want to keep this going.

Pack it out.

Trash can by the door. Cups, wrappers, bring it with you when you leave.

Be a good neighbor.

Shared lot. Other businesses open Sunday. Respect the lines.

Say hi.

To Jack, to the baristas, to the guy with the teal Thunderbird. That is the whole point.
