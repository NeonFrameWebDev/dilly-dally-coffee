# Dilly Dally Coffee Co. build notes

## Palette (sampled from the actual logo and in-store sign)

- `--pine` `#144C2F` — primary, from logo background and wall sign
- `--pine-deep` `#0B3620` — deeper shadow, section contrast
- `--cream` `#FAF7F2` — primary light surface, from logo cup and walls
- `--cream-2` `#F0E8D6` — secondary warm surface
- `--sand` `#EDB568` — accent, from the wayfinding arrow sign on the counter
- `--sand-deep` `#C89044` — hover and accent deep
- `--ink` `#0F1713` — near-black for menu board and body type on cream

## Typography

- Display: Fraunces (warm humanist serif, opsz 72 to 144, SOFT 50 for rounded edges)
- Body: Inter 400 / 500 / 600
- Meta / menu / schedule: JetBrains Mono 400 / 500
- Handwritten accent: Caveat 600 / 700 (used sparingly, chalkboard menu feel)
