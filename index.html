The story

Named after the first run Jack ever skied down Mt. Bachelor.

Dilly Dally Alley is an unofficial tree run off the Sunrise chair, threaded between Marshmallow and Rooster Tail. Banking turns, little jumps, more fun than it has any business being. A Bend classic.

In May 2023, Jack opened a shop a few miles downhill and named it after the run. The logo is the coffee cup you already knew. The wall sign says the mountains are better with coffee, and it is not wrong.

We pour Thump, brew pour overs in the morning, pull clean espresso all day, and steam a matcha latte that people drive across town for. Roasting our own is next.

Jack · Owner, since 2023

Thump Coffee · Bend

We pour Thump. We are learning to roast.

The beans on the hopper come from Thump, one of the best roasters in town. We chose them because their lightest roasts taste like fruit, their espresso blend hits bitter chocolate, and they ship fresh enough that you can date the bag by the week.

We are working toward roasting our own. A small batch, one origin, opinionated, not trying to do everything. When it is ready, the bags will be on the back shelf. Until then, we are proud to pour what we pour.

Visit

Southern Crossing, Bend. Plenty of parking.

We are on the west side of the Bend Parkway, one minute off Knott and Parkway. Business park out front, outdoor patio in back with rolling garage doors that open on warm mornings. Strong wifi, plenty of tables, laptop friendly.
