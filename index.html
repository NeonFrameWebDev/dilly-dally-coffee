why

the story, shortish

Dilly Dally Alley is an unofficial tree run off the Sunrise chair at Mt. Bachelor, threaded between Marshmallow and Rooster Tail. Banking turns, little jumps, more fun than it has any business being. A Bend classic.

In May 2023, Jack opened a shop a few miles downhill and named it after the run. The logo is the coffee cup you already knew. The sign on the wall says the mountains are better with coffee, and it is not wrong.

We pour Thump, brew pour overs in the morning, pull clean espresso all day, and steam a matcha latte that people drive across town for. Roasting our own is next.

Thump Coffee · Bend

We pour Thump. We are learning to roast.

The beans on the hopper come from Thump, one of the best roasters in town. We chose them because their light roasts taste like fruit, their espresso hits bitter chocolate, and they ship fresh enough that you can date the bag by the week.

We are working toward roasting our own. A small batch, one origin, opinionated, not trying to do everything. When it is ready, the bags will be on the back shelf.
